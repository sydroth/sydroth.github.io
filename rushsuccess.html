I contribute to citizen science projects because I believe in adding value to scientific advancement space exploration for the benefit of the field. Also, I love space. Sungrazer Project

Approved project contributors locate comet candidates in LASCO C2 and C3 telescope images and compare the speed, direction, shape, size, and brightness of the candidate over multiple image frames.
